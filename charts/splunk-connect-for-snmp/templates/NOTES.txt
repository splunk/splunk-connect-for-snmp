Version 1.6.3 of SC4SNMP enhances scalability of worker pods. If you upgraded from previous version and want to use horizontal pod autoscaling, turn on metrics-server addon of microk8s:

microk8s enable metrics-server

Make sure to update worker part in values.yaml according to the documentation:
https://splunk.github.io/splunk-connect-for-snmp/develop/configuration/worker-configuration

Values.yaml template is available here:
https://splunk.github.io/splunk-connect-for-snmp/develop/gettingstarted/sc4snmp-installation
